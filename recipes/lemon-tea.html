Lemon Tea

Description

A quick and refreshing Lemon Tea recipe that is easy to make and packed with health benefits. This lemon tea has a soothing flavor and helps with digestion and detoxification. Lemon tea can be prepared in different ways, depending on your taste preference. Whether made with fresh lemon juice or brewed with tea leaves, it is always refreshing. Learn How to Make Lemon Tea at Home in different ways.

Ingredients

  • 1 - cup Water
  • 1 - tsp Tea leaves
  • 1 - tsp Lemon juice
  • 1 - tsp Ginger
  • 1 - tsp Honey
  • 3 - Mint leaves
  • 1 - Small Cinnamon stick

Steps

  1. Heat 1 cup of water in a saucepan.
  2. Add tea leaves or a tea bag and let it steep for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Remove the tea leaves or tea bag.
  4. Mix in freshly squeezed lemon juice.
  5. Stir in honey or sugar if desired.
  6. Add ginger, mint leaves, or cinnamon for extra taste.
  7. Mix everything thoroughly.
  8. Pour into a cup and enjoy.
